DigCrate Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 12, 2026

DigCrate is built so that your music and your data stay on your Mac. This policy is short because there is very little to disclose.

What DigCrate collects

Nothing. DigCrate does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data, usage analytics, or telemetry. There are no accounts, no tracking, and no third-party SDKs.

Where your data lives

Your music library analysis (BPM, key, energy), sets, and shortlists are stored in a local database on your Mac. Audio files are read in place and never copied or uploaded.

AI set planning

Set planning runs on Apple's on-device intelligence or on a local model server you operate yourself. Track metadata is processed on your machine. If a future version offers Apple's Private Cloud Compute for planning, it will be clearly labeled, and Apple's Private Cloud Compute privacy guarantees apply: data is not stored or accessible to anyone, including Apple or us.

Discovery links

The Discover feature opens search pages on Beatport, Spotify, or SoundCloud in your web browser. The app itself sends nothing to these services; once in your browser, their privacy policies apply.

Purchases

DigCrate Pro is a one-time purchase handled entirely by Apple through the Mac App Store. We never see your payment details. See Apple's Privacy Policy.
